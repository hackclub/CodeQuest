ABOUT THE JAM

Welcome to **Cram Jam**, the ultimate challenge for aspiring and professional game developers alike! This is your chance to build a complete, fully playable web game in just **5 hours**. You'll be working against the clock to design, develop, and launch your creation, all for the chance to win an awesome reward.

Whether you're a seasoned coder or a complete beginner, this jam is designed to push your limits, spark your creativity, and help you hone your skills. Forget long, drawn-out projectsâ€”this is a sprint where every second counts.

THE RULES

  • **5-Hour Time Limit**: The timer starts the moment you begin the jam. You have exactly 5 hours to go from concept to a completed, playable web game.
  • **Web-Based Only**: Your game must be playable directly in a web browser without any special downloads or plugins.
  • **Individual Entry**: This is a solo challenge. All work must be your own.
  • **No Pre-made Assets**: You must create all your own game assets (code, art, sound) during the 5-hour window. No using pre-existing code libraries or art packs.

HOW TO PARTICIPATE

STEP 1: Register

Sign up using the form below. We'll send you a confirmation email with all the details you need.

STEP 2: The Countdown Begins

On the day of the jam, you'll receive the official start signal. The 5-hour clock starts ticking!

STEP 3: Build Your Game

Get to work! Use your favorite tools and libraries to create your masterpiece.

STEP 4: Submit

Upload your final game to our platform within the 5-hour window. Don't be late!
